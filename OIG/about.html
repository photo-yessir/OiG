Our Story

Chennai's Trusted Print Partner

OiG Print-Tech was founded with one simple goal: to provide Chennai's businesses with professional-grade printing and finishing services that are reliable, precise, and affordable.

Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted name in commercial printing and finishing. Our team brings deep expertise in paper cutting, lamination, and print finishing — handling everything from small business orders to large commercial runs.

We are proudly based in Chennai and serve clients across Tamil Nadu. Our commitment is always the same: quality output, honest pricing, and on-time delivery — every single time.
